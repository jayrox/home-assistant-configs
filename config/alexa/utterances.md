Utterances used on Alexa's API Page
----
```
LocateIntent Where is {User}
LocateIntent Where's {User}
LocateIntent Where {User} is
LocateIntent Where did {User} go

WhereAreWeIntent where we are

LivingRoomTVMuteIntent Mute the living room tv
LivingRoomTVMuteIntent Unmute the living room tv

LivingRoomTVPowerIntent Turn on the living room tv
LivingRoomTVPowerIntent Turn off the living room tv

LivingRoomTVVolUpIntent Turn up the living room tv volume
LivingRoomTVVolUpIntent Turn the volume up on the living room tv
LivingRoomTVVolDownIntent Turn down the living room tv volume
LivingRoomTVVolDownIntent Turn the volume down on the living room tv

LivingRoomTVSourceIntent Change living room tv source
```
